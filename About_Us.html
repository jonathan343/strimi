The Problem

With an increasing number of streaming platforms (Netflix, Hulu, HBO Max, Spotify, Apple Music, etc.), there are a lot more consumers transitioning from cable. Since there are more people using these services, there isn’t a centralized location to talk about your favorite music, movies, and shows. We want to create a platform for people who want to express themselves and their preferences freely with other people.

The Need

There currently is no adequate service that connects you and your friends’ media consumption. Only spotify shows what your friends are listening to currently. But all other streaming services don’t include this feature. There is also no way to see how much your friends enjoyed what they watched, what they are listening to, and their favorite artist, director, songs, shows and more. Although there are social media platforms where you can express your thoughts on content like twitter or facebook, it is easy for them to get lost in other information. There is no combined centralized social media that is dedicated to sharing and discussing music with your friends. There is a need to see what your friends are currently enjoying on all platforms on one central location and their thoughts on the content.

The Goal

We aspire to create the social media platform that centralizes where people talk about what they watch and what they listen to. On our platform we will be able to show people what their friends have consumed and if they have enjoyed them or not. People will be able to make suggestions to their friends or add media to a list watch. This will be something that facilitates people to decide what they want to watch or listen to, as well as keeping friends and family up to date on what eachother has watched so they can talk about the content or avoid spoilers. This will create a consolidated web service that includes features that will keep everyone connected for all streaming platforms
